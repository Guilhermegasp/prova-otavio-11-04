Black & White

Black and white are two colors that contrast very well with each other. Black represents darkness and mystery, while white represents light and purity. Together, these colors create a classic, timeless image that can be used in many different contexts, from fashion to decoration. In addition, black and white are also used in photography as a way to simplify and highlight the composition of an image, without the distraction of colors.

In black and white photography, contrast is particularly important as it can be used to highlight the light and dark areas of the image, creating a sense of depth and dimension.
